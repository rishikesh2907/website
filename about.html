Rashtriya Gokul Mission

Mission Overview

The Rashtriya Gokul Mission is a flagship scheme launched by the Government of India to conserve and develop indigenous cattle breeds in a focused and scientific manner.

Our AI classification system directly supports this mission by providing accurate breed identification tools that help farmers, breeders, and government agencies make informed decisions about cattle breeding and conservation.

Our Impact

Supporting Dairy Industry

By providing accurate breed identification, we help farmers make better breeding decisions that lead to improved milk productivity and genetic quality of their herds.

This directly contributes to the sustainability and growth of India's dairy sector, supporting millions of farmers and ensuring food security.

Conservation Efforts

Our system helps identify and preserve indigenous cattle breeds that are at risk of extinction, ensuring genetic diversity for future generations.

By making breed identification accessible to everyone, we democratize conservation efforts and involve the entire farming community in protecting our heritage.
